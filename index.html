Olimpiadi Di Cybersecurity

In 2022, I embarked on a journey to learn Python and explore its invaluable libraries as part of my preparation for the "Olimpiadi Di Cybersecurity," an annual competition that immerses high school students in the world of cybersecurity. Throughout the year, I enthusiastically participated in various Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, where the objective is to identify vulnerabilities and exploit them to acquire the flag, which is then submitted to the challenge's server. These challenges span a wide spectrum of topics, including scripting, network security, web security, software security, and cryptography.

You can read my repo of the problems I resolved here.
